Recipe for delicious chocolate pie

Ingredients for 4 people

  • 200 gr butter
  • 150 gr flour
  • 60 gr cocoa powder
  • 8 eggs
  • 4 sachets vanilla sugar
  • salt to taste

Steps for creating a simple, yet delicious chocolate pie!

  1. Beat the eggs with vanilla sugar to a very fluffy and airy consistency.
  2. Sieve the flour, salt and cocoa powder into one bowl.
  3. Gently fold (not whisk!) the chocolate mixture into the beaten eggs.
  4. Pour the entire mixture in a springform pan.
  5. Heat oven (175 degr. Celsius) and place the pan on a grill at the bottom of the oven.
  6. After 40 minutes, test if the pie is cooked by inserting a needle. If it comes out clean, remove pie from the oven.
  7. Let the pie cool to room temperature before slicing into pieces.

Enjoy your piece of chocolate heaven!
